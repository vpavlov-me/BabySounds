### Common Support Queries

**Q: How do I restore my premium purchase?**
A: Go to Settings > Premium > "Restore Purchases"

**Q: Why is the volume limited?**
A: We follow WHO hearing safety guidelines to protect children's hearing. You can adjust the limit in Settings > Hearing Safety (30-75%).

**Q: Can I use this offline?**
A: Yes! All sounds are included in the app. No internet required after download.

**Q: Does this work with the screen locked?**
A: Yes! Audio continues playing when your phone is locked or you're using other apps.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**
A: Go to iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > BabySounds > Cancel Subscription
