# Privacy Policy for BabySounds

**Last Updated: November 1, 2025**

## Introduction

BabySounds ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

BabySounds is designed for use with babies and young children. We are committed to compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

### Key Points:
- **We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13**
- All purchases and sensitive actions require parental verification through our Parent Gate
- We do not use children's data for advertising or marketing
- We do not share children's data with third parties
- Parents have full control over data collection and deletion

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

**Account Information** (Optional):
- Email address (if you create an account)
- Name (optional, for personalization)

**Subscription Information**:
- Purchase history (handled by Apple's StoreKit 2)
- Subscription status
- Payment is processed entirely by Apple; we never see payment card information

**User Preferences**:
- Sound favorites
- Volume preferences
- Sleep schedules
- App settings

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

**App Usage Data**:
- Sounds played and playback duration
- Features used (favorites, schedules, etc.)
- App crashes and errors
- Device type and iOS version

**Audio Listening Data**:
- Total listening time (for WHO safety compliance)
- Volume levels used
- Break recommendations triggered

### 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do NOT collect:
- Voice recordings or audio input
- Photos or camera access
- Location data
- Contacts or address book
- Health data
- Biometric data
- Any data that identifies your child personally

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

1. **App Functionality**:
   - Playing sounds and managing playback
   - Saving favorites and schedules
   - Syncing preferences across devices (if iCloud is enabled)

2. **Safety & Compliance**:
   - Enforcing WHO volume limits
   - Tracking listening time to recommend breaks
   - Preventing hearing damage

3. **Subscription Management**:
   - Processing purchases and managing subscriptions
   - Unlocking premium features
   - Handling refunds and subscription changes

4. **Analytics & Improvements**:
   - Understanding which features are used
   - Identifying and fixing bugs
   - Improving app performance
   - Developing new features

5. **Customer Support**:
   - Responding to support requests
   - Troubleshooting technical issues

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
Most of your data is stored locally on your device:
- Sound favorites
- Volume preferences
- Sleep schedules
- Listening history

### iCloud Sync (Optional)
If you enable iCloud sync:
- Preferences sync across your devices
- Data is encrypted by Apple
- You can disable iCloud sync anytime in Settings

### Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Data encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Secure storage using iOS Keychain for sensitive data
- Regular security audits
- No storage of payment information

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Services
- **StoreKit 2**: For in-app purchases and subscriptions
- **iCloud**: For optional preference syncing
- **App Store**: For app distribution

Apple's privacy policy applies to their services: [apple.com/privacy](https://www.apple.com/privacy)

### Analytics (Optional)
We may use privacy-focused analytics services like TelemetryDeck:
- No personally identifiable information collected
- Aggregated, anonymous usage statistics only
- You can opt out in Settings

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access Your Data
You can access all your data in the app Settings:
- View favorites and schedules
- Review listening history
- Check subscription status

### Delete Your Data
You can delete your data at any time:
1. Go to Settings > Privacy
2. Tap "Delete All Data"
3. Confirm deletion

This permanently deletes:
- All favorites
- All schedules
- All preferences
- All listening history

### Opt Out of Analytics
1. Go to Settings > Privacy
2. Toggle "Share Analytics" off

### Export Your Data
To request a copy of your data, contact us at: privacy@babysounds.app

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Stored until you delete it or uninstall the app
- **iCloud Data**: Stored until you delete it or disable iCloud sync
- **Purchase History**: Retained by Apple per their policies
- **Analytics**: Aggregated data retained for 2 years

## International Users

BabySounds is available worldwide. If you use our app from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where Apple operates data centers.

### GDPR Compliance (EU Users)
If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights:
- Right to access your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@babysounds.app

### CCPA Compliance (California Users)
If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):
- Right to know what data we collect
- Right to delete your data
- Right to opt out of data sales (we do not sell data)
- Right to non-discrimination
